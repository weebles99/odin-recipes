Pizza Recipe

a delicious and nutritious pizza.

absolutely stunning dish that will fill your tummy as well as your heart. enjoy the complex flavors and nuanced ingredient choice. and its so easy to make! yum!

Ingredients

  • dough
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. roll out dough
  2. add sauce
  3. add cheese
  4. add toppings
  5. bake until desired doneness
