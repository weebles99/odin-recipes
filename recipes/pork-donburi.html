Pork Donburi

Pork Donburi Recipe

a delicious and nutritious pork chop on top of a bed of rice with green onions, lettuce, tomato, garlic, and ginger.

absolutely stunning dish that will fill your tummy as well as your heart. enjoy the complex flavors and nuanced ingredient choice. and its so easy to make! yum!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless pork loin chops
  • 1 4th a tsp kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp potato starch
  • 1 Tbsp neutral oil

    • for the donburi bowl

  • 2 leaves iceberg lettuce
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 servings cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • 2 cherry tomatoes

    • for the seasonings

  • 1 knob ginger
  • 2 Tbsp sake
  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • half tsp toasted sesame oil

Steps

  1. Before You Start: Gather all the ingredients. For the steamed rice, please note that 1½ cups (300 g, 2 rice cooker cups) of uncooked Japanese short-grain rice yield 4⅓ cups (660 g) of cooked rice, enough for 2 donburi servings
  2. Cut 2 leaves iceberg lettuce into julienne strips and 2 green onions/scallions into thin rounds.
  3. Slice 2 cloves garlic and grate 1 knob ginger.
  4. In a bowl, combine the seasonings ingredients: the grated ginger, 2 Tbsp sake, 2 Tbsp rice vinegar (unseasoned), 3 Tbsp soy sauce, 2 Tbsp sugar, and ½ tsp toasted sesame oil. Whisk it all together.
